FUJI Silvertech offers durable water and sewage solutions in precast concrete, which increases construction efficiency tenfold. Our precast drainage components ensure speedy construction that leads to minimal public disturbance and are made in a controlled environment at our manufacturing plants, ensuring premium quality and minimal on-site waste.

Roads & Bridges

As a whole-range supplier of prefabricated concrete structures, we have designed, manufactured, and delivered numerous precast roads and bridges in India. Precasting these structures offsite significantly decrease construction time and costs while dramatically increasing on-site construction convenience and durability. Our solutions not only move transportation infrastructure forward in astoundingly short timelines but also ensure better construction safety norms and minimal road and traffic disruption.

Railways

We have developed precast concrete solutions for the western dedicated freight corridor and the national high speed rail as established pioneers in the railway infrastructure sector. These premium precast concrete solutions are designed to railway load-bearing specifications and shorten project timelines significantly. They ensure public convenience, a long service life, and are more cost-effective than the conventional alternative.
